Salad is better than Soup

Author: Eric Arbour

Soup isn't that good of a food. It's high in salt and is basically water. Salad is usually raw vegetables which are healthy and you get to choose any of the number of phenomenal salad dressings when you eat it. Therefore, salad is better than soup.
